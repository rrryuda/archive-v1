Blind onboarding 2.0

Overview

Improvements to Blind's onboarding screen were already planned, aiming to enhance user signup rates, user convenience, and strengthen the brand identity. The goal was to explore methods that could improve the overall user experience and contribute to the brand's identity.

As-is: Blind 2.0

Upon analyzing user data, it was identified that several guide texts on the existing Blind onboarding screen were not functioning correctly. Consequently, a plan was devised to remove unnecessary components and restructure the screen by reassessing the priority of each feature.

What we changed

During the update of the existing Blind onboarding screen, the focus shifted towards highlighting the most prioritized user options. Additionally, the incorporation of Blind's unique patterns and catchphrases through brand plays effectively enhanced the brand identity.

To-be: Blind 3.0

Visual variants comparison

Sign up UX

The new sign-up process emphasizes a screen where users can use their company email. For users who prefer to sign up with a personal email, guidance is provided explaining the differences between personal and company emails, encouraging them to reconsider and opt for the company email once again.

Performance
